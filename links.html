Bike Park — Sun Peaks Resort
With 2,000 vertical feet of lift accessed terrain, and 29 distinct trails that range from technical DH to flowing machine-made to old school single track, the Sun Peaks BC Mountain Biking Park offers something for everyone.
Silver Star Mountain Resort
Silver Star has a mountain biking park that is based on progression. Everything from beginner trails to expert runs filled with berms, table tops and a ton of wooden features are offered.
Mountain Biking BC
The Western Canada Mountain Bike Tourism Association developed Mountainbikingbc.com to identify the best mountain biking trails for mountain bikers planning to visit BC.
